Freedom Project

This project is a year long project that students in the 10th grade SEP program worked on. This project showcases what us students learned from HTML and CSS at the beginning of the year to Bootstrap, and even our own learning tools. From this, I made a website about the benefits of Martial Arts.

During the creation of this project, we went over what we call Part A, and Part B of the website. Part A was the brainstorming of the ideas, as well as what you were incorperating into your website. After that, we came into the more challenging part of the website, which was Part B, which mainly consisted of the learning of our tools. This was most difficult for me due to the fact that it was almost like learning an entire new coding language from scratch. With my tool being jQuery, I had to learn how to write a simplified version of Javascript, which took time and almost didn't work out in the end. However, after trial and error, it was finished.
